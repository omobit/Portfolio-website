About me

My story

Hi, my name is Omolade and I'm a User Experience Designer based in Manchester, UK. I bring a unique blend of passion and dedication to UX design, with a strong policy, campaigning, and advocacy background. I have a proven track record of developing and executing successful campaigns that drive social change. My skills lie in identifying areas to foster positive relationships between organizations and key stakeholders and working collaboratively with those with lived experience to bring forward change. Driven to understand user behaviour, needs, and the challenges marginalised communities face, I pursued the Careerfoundry UX design course. This allows me to develop innovative solutions centred on user experience I possess excellent communication skills, honed through delivering presentations, talks, and discussions online and in person. I've previously appeared on BBC, ITV News, and Associated Press, discussing various topics such as race, gender, and mental well-being
